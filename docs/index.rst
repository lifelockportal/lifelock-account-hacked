What Is a Hacked LifeLock Account?
==================================

A hacked LifeLock account is an account that has been accessed or controlled by someone without your permission. Unauthorized users may attempt to change account information, access personal details, or interfere with your account settings.

Recovering a hacked account involves securing your login credentials, verifying your identity, reviewing account activity, and restoring full control of your account.

Signs Your LifeLock Account May Be Hacked
=========================================

You may notice several warning signs if your account has been compromised.

Common signs include:

* Unable to sign in using your usual password
* Password has been changed without your permission
* Unknown account changes
* Unrecognized login activity
* Unexpected security alerts
* Changes to account information
* Suspicious emails related to your account
* Unknown devices accessing your account

Recognizing these signs early can help you take action before additional damage occurs.

Why LifeLock Accounts May Be Targeted
====================================

Identity protection accounts may be targeted because they contain valuable personal and account-related information.

Common reasons include:

* Weak or reused passwords
* Phishing attempts
* Malware or unsafe software
* Unauthorized access to email accounts
* Using unsecured networks
* Sharing login information
* Outdated security settings

Understanding these risks helps users take preventive measures.

How to Recover a Hacked LifeLock Account
========================================

Secure Your Email Account First
-------------------------------

If your email account is connected to your LifeLock account, secure it first by changing the email password and enabling additional security features if available.

Attempt to Sign In
------------------

Try signing in using your regular account credentials to determine whether you still have access.

Reset Your Password
-------------------

If your password no longer works, use the password recovery process to create a new secure password.

Complete Identity Verification
------------------------------

Follow the required verification steps to confirm that you are the authorized account owner.

Review Account Information
--------------------------

Check your account details for unauthorized changes, including contact information and security settings.

Remove Unknown Access
---------------------

Review connected devices or account activity and remove unfamiliar access where possible.

Update Your Security Information
--------------------------------

Change your password and update recovery information to prevent further unauthorized access.

Monitor Your Account Activity
-----------------------------

Continue reviewing your account for unusual activity after recovery.

Contact Official Support if Needed
----------------------------------

If you cannot recover access through standard methods, additional verification may be required through official support channels.
